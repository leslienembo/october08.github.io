    Signals 2 Noise: Arts, Aesthetics and Social Practice of Community Radios from Eastern, Central and Northern Europe

    Signals2Noise is an 18-hour festival celebrating the art, aesthetics and social practice of 16 community radios from Central, Eastern and Northern Europe. Taking place on October 4 2024 in Berlin, S2N presents live music, radiophonic interventions, installations, talks, DJ sets, filming screenings, workshops and more.

    In times of an increasingly fragmented media landscape, and resurgence of censorship and obstruction of free access to information by state and corporate actors, community radio keeps diverse voices heard. S2N aims to amplify these voices, and support new and existing connections among radios and their audiences.

    S2N is hosted by the Berlin-based experimental community radio Cashmere Radio, in cooperation with the online platform Easterndaze. It follows 3 in-person exchanges between community radios in Spring 2024 , presenting their contributions and alongside guest speakers and performers.
